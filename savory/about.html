About us

Our story

RecipeSearch is a team of UNSW students currently in their second year of Software Engineering. This application was created as a project solution for SENG2021, 18s1.

When examining the initial specification statement for the project, our team looked to our own lives, as sleep-deprived, poor university students, in order to find a problenm worth solving. We soon found that majority of the team had either very limited cooking experience/knowledge or simply did not have the time or money to cook for themselves. Thus, our idea for RecipeSearch was born, an application that aims to make cooking easier, cheaper and more efficient. Because we believe that everyone should be able to cook for themselves.
