What is RecipeSearch?

Too busy to cook? Don't know what to cook? Don't have the budget to cook? Meet RecipeSearch.

Save time

Using our intuitive search engine as well as our weekly meal planner, you can make cooking a quick and painless process to introduce into your weekly schedule.

Get inspiration

We take the guesswork out of choosing recipes to cook. Provide us with a list of inredients, a meal you want to make, a cuisine type or a dietary requirement to find recipes curated by us.

Save money

Our tools help you find recipes to cook with the ingredients you have, as well as helping you spend less on groceries by planning out shopping lists based on meals.
